MEMEMORIES

▶

The game Mememories is about the protagonist being in a state of losing one of the human's key abilities his memory. In this game, the protagonist is sent to a maze where he is allowed to regain his memories by interacting with the other NPC characters (his family members) and collecting short instances of his life from each of them in the form of short clips which are also to be referred as memes.

SNOW DODGE

▶

"SNOW DODGE" is an engaging survival game where players control a character to dodge falling blocks. The challenge intensifies as the blocks fall faster, requiring quick reflexes and sharp timing. The goal is simple: avoid collisions and survive as long as possible. As difficulty increases, the game tests the player's focus and agility, making for an exciting and fast-paced experience.

ST. DYMPHNA'S HOSPITAL

▶

ST.DYMPHNA'S HOSPITAL Players assume the role of an investigator exploring the eerie St. Dymphna’s Hospital, once renowned for mental health treatments but now shrouded in mystery. Tasked with uncovering patient files, they navigate haunting corridors filled with whispers and shadows that reveal the tragic tales of “The Forgotten Patients.” As they piece together the hospital's dark history, players face malevolent spirits guarding its secrets, where every choice blurs the line between reality and nightmare. Objective: Uncover the truth behind the hospital’s tragic history and escape the restless spirits that linger within its walls.
